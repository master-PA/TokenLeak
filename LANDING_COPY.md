## FAQ
1. **Is this free?**
   Yes. TokenLeak is completely free to use. You don't even need to create an account or provide a credit card to run the audit.

2. **Do you need my email?**
   No. We don't ask for your email until *after* you see your savings. If you want a PDF copy of the report or want to talk to us about capturing those savings, you can optionally provide your email at the end.

3. **How accurate are the savings?**
   Highly accurate. Our engine calculates savings using the official, up-to-date pricing pages of every vendor we support. We don't invent savings—if your stack is optimal, we'll tell you.

4. **Is my data public?**
   Only if you choose to share your unique URL. By default, your audit is private to your browser session. If you generate a share link, identifying details (like your email or company name) are automatically stripped from the public version.

5. **What happens if I save >$500/mo?**
   If we identify significant savings (>$500/mo), we'll highlight an option for you to book a free consultation with Credex. We can often help you capture even more of those savings through discounted infrastructure credits.
